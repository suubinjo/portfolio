Overview

Designing beyond the game

We partnered with Electronic Arts for this industry-sponsored capstone to explore how AI could shape second-screen experiencesWhat are second-screen experiences?Second-screen experiences are the apps and platforms players use outside the game while they play. for sports video games.

Rather than designing for a specific title, we started with an open-ended challenge: understand how players engage with digital experiences before, during, and after gameplay. Through interviews, concept exploration, and iterative testing, we identified an opportunity within EA SPORTS FC 26 Career Mode and designed an AI-powered companion app that extends the immersive experience beyond the game.

The Problem

How might we design AI-powered second-screen experiences that help players interpret gameplay, reflect on performance, and build stronger connections to their club journey?

To improve their gameplay, many players rely on Discord, YouTube, Reddit, companion apps, and stat websites to scout players, compare tactics, learn mechanics, and seek advice from the community. We found examples of many dedicated fans creating spreadsheets and systems to track their career mode gameplay.

While these resources are valuable, they exist across separate platforms, requiring players to constantly switch contexts and interpret information on their own. We saw an opportunity to explore how AI could bring these experiences together into a more connected decision-support experience.
