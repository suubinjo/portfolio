Overview

Making wardrobe decisions feel lighter.

The Problem

People own enough clothes, but still feel unsure what to wear.

Research

We studied outfit routines, weather context, and style memory.

Ideation

Concepts explored manual selection, saved outfits, and smart recommendations.

Solution

A closet assistant that turns existing items into clear outfit options.

Delivery

Interactive prototype and final presentation.

Reflection

Designing for confidence means reducing decisions, not adding more.
